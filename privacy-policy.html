Cookie Inspector Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

Introduction

Cookie Inspector ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users inspect, add, edit, and delete cookies within their browser. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

Information Collection and Usage

What We DO NOT Collect:

The Cookie Inspector extension:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • Does NOT track your browsing history
  • Does NOT store any cookie data outside your browser
  • Does NOT contain any analytics or tracking code
  • Does NOT use any third-party services

Local Operations Only:

All operations performed by the Extension (including viewing, adding, editing, and deleting cookies) are executed entirely within your browser. No data is sent to external servers or stored outside of your local browser environment.

Permissions:

The Extension requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Cookie access: To read and modify browser cookies
  • Active tab access: To interact with cookies from the current website
  • Storage permission: To save extension settings locally in your browser

These permissions are used solely for the Extension's core functionality and not for data collection.

Data Security

Since we do not collect, transmit, or store any data from your browser, there are no security concerns related to data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our Extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this document with a new "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be announced through the Extension's update notes.
